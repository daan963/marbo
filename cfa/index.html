Boston University College of Fine Arts

A world leader in the arts since 1872

The Schools of CFA: The Ultimate Training Ground

School of Music

BU School of Music offers an unparalleled experience, preparing students for diverse and impactful professional careers. The first degree-granting music school in the United States, the School of Music continues to lead the field.

Read More

School of Theatre

BU School of Theatre is a nationally recognized program with all the opportunities of a larger University. We believe each student should pursue their individual interests while receiving strong foundational techniques.

Read More

School of Visual Arts

At BU School of Visual Arts, we nurture the creative potential of every student through a balance of rigorous training and open exploration. Through our ethos and curriculum, SVA engages students in highly experiential learning.

Read More

CFA DEGREES & PROGRAMS

With more than 30 programs of study, on-campus and online modalities, and a full range of undergraduate and graduate options, CFA educates artists, musicians, performers, scholars, and educators at all stages of their professional careers.

Who Are We?

With programs in Music, Theatre, and Visual Arts, Boston University College of Fine Arts offers a wide array of pre-college, undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs, as well as a range of online degrees and certificates.
