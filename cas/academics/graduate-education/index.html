Boston University Arts & Sciences is a multidisciplinary institution where students and faculty are working and studying across the humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and mathematical and computational sciences to understand our present and past, explore unanswered questions, discover creative solutions, and take knowledge, ideas, and experience into action.
