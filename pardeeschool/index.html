At the Pardee School, we cultivate an inclusive community where you thrive academically, professionally, and personally. Welcoming Our Diverse and Global 2024 Graduate Student Cohort

As we kick off a new academic year, we're excited to introduce our newest graduate student cohort!
Representing 16 countries including Turkey, India, China, France, Indonesia, and the US, this group brings an incredible range of experiences and knowledge to our school. With languages spoken ranging from Mandarin to French, Russian to Korean, and Nepali to Spanish, our students truly reflect a global perspective.

Their academic backgrounds are just as diverse, spanning fields like International Relations, Political Science, History, and Economics, alongside perspectives from Engineering, Mathematics, Gender Studies, Public Relations, and more.

This new cohort is ready to make a meaningful impact, and we look forward to seeing their contributions in the years to come.
