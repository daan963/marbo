    Browse Our Programs

    Aquatics

    Jump in! Our facility features two swimming pools, and we offer classes for all levels of swimming for adults and children, plus certifications. Both pools are available for open swim for FitRec members and BU students.

    Outdoor Programs

    Whether it is an outdoor skills workshop, a local hike, paddle or climb around Boston, or a multi-day backpacking trip in the mountains, Outdoor Programs has something for everyone. And don't forget to visit our 30-foot climbing wall.

    Club & Intramural Sports

    BU has student teams in more than 30 club sports, from fencing, to table tennis, to triathlons. Or join one of our popular intramural leagues, open year-round to BU students, faculty, and staff, plus alumni in the summer months.

    Fitness

    FitRec has the most comprehensive, best-equipped facilities around. Plus, a diverse range of classes, from HIIT to Yoga. We also offer various certifications and personal training.

    Dance

    Whatever your passion, from learning to performing to attending events, we have something to offer. You can take our classes for academic credit and/or as recreational dance classes. A dance minor is also available for undergraduates.

    Emergency Medical Response

    We offer a full range of courses in Emergency Medical Services that are open to both the BU community and the general public.

    Sports

    Try out Tae Kwon Do, tennis, squash, and volleyball.

    Certifications

    We offer a variety of certification programs to help you reach your career and personal goals.
