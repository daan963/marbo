Situated on the banks of the Charles River, Boston University School of Theology is the oldest United Methodist seminary in North America, and we have prepared leaders to make a positive difference for nearly two centuries. No matter where you are in the world, take a moment to visit our campus – and picture yourself as part of our beloved community.
