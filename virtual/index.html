Our Programs

Virtual

Welcome to a powerful combination of convenience and academic excellence.

With BU Virtual, you’ll actively participate in a highly engaging and interactive learning experience, collaborating with a community of students and faculty who contribute a range of insights and backgrounds. As a leading institution recognized for top-tier research and education, our online programs blend course study and real-world experiences to create opportunities to apply what you learn in your career and in your community.


Imagine the impact
you can make on the world.

Explore our expanding portfolio of online programs, developed in collaboration with top tier faculty and researchers at Boston University.

The work we are doing today will shape the future of global online education tomorrow. We believe that our work at BU Virtual, which is done in collaboration with our award-winning schools and colleges, brings a new level of innovation, reach, and access to learners around the globe and reinforces our commitment to inclusion at scale. —Wendy Colby, VP and Associate Provost, BU Virtual

No matter where you are in your life, geography, or career, BU Virtual will help you develop and enhance world-class skills and experiences to succeed in a dynamic and rapidly evolving labor market. And, we provide the unparalleled competitive and reputational advantages that come from being a part of the Boston University global community.

Boston University on Your Terms

Boston University Virtual meets you where you are today and throughout any stage of your career. We are innovators, educators, entrepreneurs, and university leaders seeking to bring BU to the service of the world and the world closer to the vibrant BU community.

Academic Excellence and Learning Innovation

Earn a top-ranked online master’s degree or certificate—a robust, rigorous, and engaging learning experience with faculty and peers incorporating the latest technologies and tools.

Leading Faculty and Researchers

Engage with our world-acclaimed faculty and researchers who also teach on our campus in Boston; they bring the same level of innovation, exchange, and discovery to our online program experiences.

Global Community

Join a welcoming global community of faculty and students who bring a rich depth of life along with work experience from some of the leading companies impacting change across the world.

Our Programs

Boston University Virtual works in close collaboration with the schools and colleges across BU to provide innovative, high-quality online programs in areas ranging from business and public health to social work, occupational therapy, education, computer science, and more.

Online Master of Public Health

Online Master of Data Science

Boston University’s online Master's in Data Science program is positioned to meet the ever-growing demand for data science and artificial intelligence (AI) skills. Offered by the Faculty of Computing and Data Science, it teaches key skills and knowledge through experiential, professionally-relevant classes and assessment.

Read More

Information Security

Government agencies, hospitals, insurance companies, private businesses. They all have increasingly complex digital ecosystems, demanding constant vigilance in their cybersecurity measures. BU’s Metropolitan College offers an Information Security Certificate that introduces you to various aspects of cybersecurity, including IT security policies and system security.

Read More

Software Engineering

As a skilled software engineer, you will be in high demand. The Software Engineering Certificate from BU’s Metropolitan College delivers advanced knowledge of software projects, including requirements analysis, design patterns, testing and maintenance, and project management and design for enterprise software applications.

Read More

Enterprise Risk Management

Addressing unforeseen challenges in today’s world is the focus of the Enterprise Risk Management Certificate, providing expertise in all areas of planning and strategy plus the reactive aspects of crisis management and enterprise and organizational continuity. You'll gain exposure to the technical and logistical issues facing emergency management professionals, while understanding the psychological and social components as well.
