What We Do

At BU Virtual, we understand the importance of high-quality online education to meet learners where they are in their lives and in their careers.

We take this mission seriously—working to bring the best of BU to the world and in ways that impact the greatest number of students.

In collaboration with Boston University’s faculty and researchers, we develop fully accredited online graduate degree and certificate programs that are designed to help solve important global challenges. We continue to adapt, enhance, and align our graduate online programs to ensure we are delivering rigorous, relevant, engaging programs that reflect the real needs of learners and enable them to effectively advance and excel in their careers.

Our work will continue to expand and evolve as we explore the best ways to impact and transform the changing world of online higher education. We will continue to be a strong voice and advocate for high-quality, affordable, and scalable education that builds on the reputation, research, and success of Boston University.
