ABOUT KILACHAND

Kilachand offers an integrated, four-year curriculum that includes experiential, team learning, and co-curricular opportunities and the infrastructure to engage in a sustained experience of intellectual discovery through the Keystone Project. Kilachand students leave Boston University with the capacity to understand and confront major challenges as they affirmatively and skillfully engage with diverse social, cultural, scientific, and philosophical perspectives.

Learn More

OUR THREE PILLARS

Knowledge

Kilachand Honors College offers a challenging liberal education grounded in exploration, discovery, creativity, and the real-world application of knowledge.

Learn More

Humanity

Kilachand students address important global, social, corporate, and geopolitical challenges as they seek to understand the shared conditions of our humanity.

Learn More

Community

Through co-curricular and social events, personal and professional development opportunities, and empowerment spaces, we invite the full participation of all students in our diverse community.
