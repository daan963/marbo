Engaging with and changing the world is part of Boston University’s identity. We welcome thousands of students and scholars from around the world to our three campuses in Boston. Our students, faculty, and staff also pursue research, engage in service, and commit to their studies in locations around the globe. BU Global Programs helps to make it all possible and to supports their efforts.

Above are some amazing images of places our students have explored, click on the image to read more. Or explore more of the Global Study and Research going on at BU.

If you want start your own international activity, learn about resources Global Programs offers to assist your interests abroad or explore resources we have created to help our international students adjust to life at BU, continue to our Engage section. Currently managing an overseas activity or about to embark on one? You’ll find plenty of helpful information in the Manage section.
